Margarita Pizza

How to make

Margherita Pizza

-1/2 recipe homemade pizza dough
-1 tablespoon olive oil
-2 cloves roasted garlic, finely chopped
-1/4 cup your favorite pizza or tomato sauce
-8 ounces mozzarella cheese, sliced into 1/2 inch thick pieces
-2 plum tomatoes, sliced (or any tomato you like)
-Handful of fresh basil
-Fresh ground black pepper, to taste

Peppetoni Pizza

How to make

Pepperoni Pizza

-1/2 recipe homemade pizza dough
-1 teaspoons olive oil
-1 clove garlic , finely minced
-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
-1/4 cup finely shredded parmesan cheese
-20 slices pepperoni (I recommend turkey, it's just as good as the regular)
-2 teaspoons fresh oregano (chopped if using larger leaves)
-Red pepper flakes , for serving (optional)

Meat-Lover’s Pizza

How to make

Meat-Lover’s Pizza

-1/2 recipe homemade pizza dough
-1/2 recipe homemade pizza sauce (or you can use store-bought)
-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
-1/3 cup shredded parmesan cheese
-Pepperoni slices
-200g hot sausage, cooked and crumbled
-3,4 slices of crispy bacon, crumbled

BBQ Chicken Pizza

How to make

BBQ Chicken Pizza

-1/2 recipe homemade pizza dough
-1,2 teaspoons olive oil
-1/3 cup + 2 tablespoons your favorite BBQ sauce
-1 cup chopped or shredded cooked chicken (about one 8-ounce breast)
-2/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
-2/3 cup shredded smoked gouda cheese
-1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
-Chopped fresh cilantro for topping(optional)

Extra Cheese Pizza

How to make

Extra Cheese Pizza

-1/2 recipe homemade pizza dough
-1/2–3/4 cup (127-190g) homemade pizza sauce -8 ounces sliced mozzarella cheese
-1 and 1/2 cups (168g) shredded mozzarella cheese
-2,3 tablespoons (10-15g) grated parmesan cheese
-Ground basil to taste

Hawaiian Pizza

How to make

Hawaiian Pizza

-1/2 recipe homemade pizza dough
-1/2 cup (127g) pizza sauce (homemade or store-bought)
-1 and 1/2 cups (6oz or 168g) shredded mozzarella cheese
-1/2 cup (75g) cooked ham or Canadian bacon, sliced or chopped
-1/2 cup (82g) pineapple chunks (canned or fresh)
-3 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
